About DMLUC Cooperative

History of DMLUC Cooperative

It was in 1995 when DMMMSU President Dr. Manuel Corpuz sent Dr. Concepcion Bederio to attend a summer institure on Local Government Administration in UP Diliman, Quezon City.


It was during the summer institute when Dr, Bederio was exposed to progressive cooperative specially the cooperative of Bulacan.


After the summer institute Dr. C. Bederio shared the idea to the dean of graduate school who was then Dr. Bugaoan. He approved the idea that was presented to graduate studies faculty. Fortunately the faculty accepted it. However they were only four so the faculty members of the Midwifery Department and College of Education were also recruited.


Mr. Aguilos of the Department of Agriculture and Mr. Flores of the Municipal cooperative were invited to talk on the organization of the cooperative.


The DMMMSU Multipurpose Cooperative Incorporated was organized with only 28 members with share capital of Php. 20,000.


The Members the Board of Directors were Dr. M. Caoile, Mrs. E. Aprecio, Dr. C. Manzano, Dr. Mendoza and Dr. Oliver with Dr. C. Bederio as the Manager with two full time employee.


In 2007 , the name was changed to DMMMSU La Union Multipurpose Cooperative because members was extended to the whole DMMMSU North La Union Campus, South La Union Campus and Mid La Union faculty and staff could become a member.


In 2011 the DMMMSU La Union Multipurpose cooperative was changed from institutional to residential cooperative, to expand membership to the whole region.


At present DLUMC have more than 200 members with share deposit of more than Php.6, 000,000.00. Pursuant to R.A. 6938 on the Cooperative of the Philippines and R.A. 6939 on the Cooperative Development Authority the DLUMC was formed and registered on 1995 at the Cooperative Development Authority City of San Fernando, La Union, Region 1 (CDA-R1)
